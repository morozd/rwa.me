What is Flitsmeister?

Flitsmeister is a real-time traffic analysis app for The Netherlands, powered by its users. Together with Sjoerd Perfors I founded it in 2009 and in 2012 we transformed it into a profitable business together with Jaco de Vries of Omnext. Since January 2014 we have over 500.000 active users and our Flitsmeister apps can be downloaded on all the major platforms. Flitsmeister is the most well-known name in traffic in The Netherlands right now. The app was featured in Top Gear Magazine in 2010 and has been mentioned on almost all the dutch radio stations, websites and magazines.

How it started

In 2009, my colleague Sjoerd Perfors and I were frustrated that there were no traffic apps for The Netherlands that showed where there were speedtraps, traffic jams and speed cameras. So we decided to build one ourselves. After 6 months of designing and developing after our day job and during the weekends, we where finished with our first version. We decided we wanted to release it to the public and submitted it to the App Store in the beginning of 2010. What happened next was something we would have never expected. People started downloading the app and a few years later we reached half a million active users.

Together with Sjoerd Perfors, Jaco de Vries, Jorn de Vries, Ruben Meijering, Ruben Ernst and Nick Meessen we're working every day to improve our traffic analysis service through our apps and website. We're working closely with some of the biggest companies in our country to make sure we keep improving our growth rate and reach as many people as we can.

As a co-founder, not only have I been responsible for a lot of the design and development work, together with Sjoerd Perfors and Jaco de Vries I'm also responsible for making the bigger decisions through our management team.
