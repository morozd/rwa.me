About me

My name is Rick Waalders and I am a designer with an engineering background. I finished a computer science degree to become a developer, but the design part of building a product has always been my biggest passion. I love finding that sweet spot of applications that aren't just looking great, but are also very easy to use.

I post design work and interact with other designers on Dribbble. You can find me on Twitter or contact me through email.

As a developer, I've built applications in Objective-C, C#, Java, PHP and of course Javascript, HTML and CSS. Every once in a while I write some Ruby too.

Simplicity is about subtracting the obvious, and adding the meaningful.
John Maeda - The Laws Of Simplicity

I love to build products that help people. I've co-founded Flitsmeister with Sjoerd Perfors and started GoVisual with him and Jaco de Vries while working at Omnext. I recently built Dragshare for Dropbox. You can find more of my personal projects in the work section of this site.

Some history

I started developing and designing websites in 1998. In 2000, I co-founded and built one of the biggest dutch music websites called 'Clubcharts'. When I finished school in 2007, I started working at Omnext, where I've worked with bigger companies like IBM.

Omnext is also the company where I met Sjoerd Perfors, with whom I started Flitsmeister, and Jaco de Vries who joined us together with Jorn de Vries to help us shape Flitsmeister into a profitable business.

The last couple of years I have been focusing on designing for mobile and I've worked closely with several small startups, big companies but also individuals from all over the globe.
