The Secret Handshake

February 11, 2014

A few weeks ago I stumbled upon a site called ‘The Secret Handshake’. It’s a site with advice from a lot of my favorite designers, on topics like creating your Resume, the Application Process and tips on what makes a great Portfolio. I find myself visiting it at least once a week, clicking random photos to learn and be inspired.
