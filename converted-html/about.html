About me

My name is Rick Waalders and I am a designer with an engineering background. I finished a computer science degree to become a developer, but the design part of building a product has always been my biggest passion. I love finding that sweet spot of applications that aren't just looking great, but are also very easy to use.

I post design work and interact with other designers on Dribbble. You can also find me on Twitter.

As a developer, I build applications in Objective-C, C#, PHP and of course Javascript, HTML and CSS. Every once in a while I write some Ruby too.

The entrepeneur in me is always finding a way to build products that help people. A lot of my time is spent building Flitsmeister (which I co-founded with Sjoerd Perfors) and Dragshare for Dropbox (which is one of my personal projects). More of my personal projects can be found in the work section of this website.

Some history

I started developing and designing websites in 1998. In 2000, I co-founded and built one of the biggest dutch music websites called 'Clubcharts'. When I finished school in 2007, I started working at Omnext, where I've worked with bigger companies like IBM. At Omnext is where I met Sjoerd Perfors, who I started Flitsmeister with, and CEO Jaco de Vries who later joined to help us with the business side of things. The last couple of years I have been focusing on designing for mobile and I've worked closely with several small startups, big companies but also individuals from all over the globe.
